About

The Department of Computer Science at the University of Karachi, founded in 1984, is a leading institution offering comprehensive programs in Computer Science, from BS to PhD in the field of Computer Science and Software Engineering. With a focus on advanced research and practical skills, the department aims to produce top-tier professionals to meet industry demands. Strong connections with national and international organizations ensure that both faculty and students stay aligned with global academic and professional standards.

Career Connect

Career Connect is based on the primary goal of merging academia with the working environment by establishing connections between emerging employees and aspiring graduates who wish to be career-oriented. The event, held annually at the Department of Computer Science, University of Karachi, provides a pivotal platform for aspiring graduates and ambitious professionals to interact and create a nurturing environment.

Attendees will engage in multiple activities that enhance skills required in their newly chosen professions.

Venue: Department of Computer Science, UBIT Garden, University of Karachi
