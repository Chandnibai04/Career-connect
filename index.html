Invitation

The Department of Computer Science at the University of Karachi is pleased to invite you to "Career Connect & Alumni Dinner 2024," a gathering that brings together industry professionals, students, and alumni, fostering meaningful connections and collaborations across the community. At Career Connect 2024, your organization will have the opportunity to engage with emerging talent from the Department of Computer Science, eager to contribute and grow within their chosen fields. Involvement in this event helps cultivate relationships and provides participants with valuable insights into professional life. Later in the evening, the Alumni Annual Dinner 2024 will be held that will offer an informal setting to reconnect with alumni, faculty, and current students. This event celebrates shared experiences and ideas, highlighting the connections that have strengthened over the years. Your presence would support the next generation of professionals and contribute to a lasting sense of community, making the occasion truly special. Looking forward to welcoming you to this meaningful event.
